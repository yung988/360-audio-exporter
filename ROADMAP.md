# Roadmap

## v0.1

- Native SwiftUI macOS app.
- Export 360 video through ffmpeg.
- Transfer or replace audio tracks on finished videos (Audio Transfer).
- Show source metadata, stream info, progress, ETA, and post-export validation.

## Next

- Better ffmpeg capability detection for codec/container compatibility.
- Optional bundled ffmpeg builds or first-run setup assistant.
- More detailed progress stages for multi-pass or long exports.
- Output command preview for advanced users.
- Spherical metadata injection workflow.
- Signed and notarized macOS releases.

## Out of Scope for Now

- Timeline editing.
- Trimming.
- Color grading.
- Waveform editing.
- Custom video decoder.
- Camera stitching engine.
